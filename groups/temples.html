Temples

The many temples of Waterdeep are some of the most breathtaking sights in the city. I have listed the oldest and most grand locations below, which are definitely a worthwhile visit regardless of one's own faith. For the pious looking for their own congregation, these temples cover a variety of dieties and are always welcoming newcomers. There are also many smaller religious communities-too many for me to list here!

More detailed descriptions of a temple can be found in its associated ward section.
